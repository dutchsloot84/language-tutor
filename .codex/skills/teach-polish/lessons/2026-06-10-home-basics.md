# Lesson: Home Basics You Can Use Today

## Situation
Moving through a normal morning at home with short, useful Polish.

## Target Phrases
| Polish | English | Pronunciation | Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Pomóż mi, proszę. | Help me, please. | POH-moosh mee PROH-sheh | Soft request |
| Posprzątajmy razem. | Let’s clean up together. | poh-spzhon-TAI-mih RAH-zem | Good with kids |
| Odłóż to na miejsce. | Put that back in its place. | OD-woozh toh nah MYES-tseh | Direct but normal |
| Kocham cię. | I love you. | KOH-ham chyeh | Daily affection |
| Poczekaj chwilę. | Wait a moment. | poh-CHEH-kai HFEE-leh | Useful boundary |
| Zaraz wracam. | I’ll be right back. | ZAH-rahs VRAH-tsam | Reassurance |
| Chwileczkę. | Just a second. | hfee-LECH-keh | Casual |
| Już idę. | I’m coming. | yoosh EE-deh | When called |
| Dzisiaj zostajemy w domu. | Today we’re staying home. | jee-SHAI zoh-stah-YEH-mih v DOH-moo | Plans |

## Short Explanation
Start with phrases that can be used as whole chunks. Do not worry about building every sentence from grammar rules yet.

## Pattern to Notice
Commands like `odłóż`, `poczekaj`, and `chodź` are direct. Add `proszę` when you want a softer family tone.

## Mini Quiz
1. How do you say "Help me, please"?
2. What does `Chwileczkę` mean?
3. Fill in: `Odłóż to na ____`.

## Speaking Drill
1. Say "Help me, please" three times.
2. Say "Put that back in its place" while pointing at an object.
3. Say "I love you" naturally, not like a vocabulary word.

## Review Flashcards
- Pomóż mi, proszę.
- Odłóż to na miejsce.
- Chwileczkę.
- Dzisiaj zostajemy w domu.

## Use This Today
Use at least 3 home phrases today and write one note about what felt natural or hard.

## Log Afterward

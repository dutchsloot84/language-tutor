# Drill: Repair Phrases With My Wife

## Weak Area
Staying in Polish when I do not understand.

## Tiny Explanation
Repair phrases are conversation tools. They prevent one missed word from ending the Polish exchange.

## Examples
| Polish | English | Pronunciation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Nie rozumiem jeszcze. | I don’t understand yet. | nyeh roh-ZOO-myem YESH-cheh |
| Możesz powiedzieć wolniej? | Can you say it slower? | MOH-zhesh poh-VYEH-jetch VOL-nyei |
| Możesz powtórzyć? | Can you repeat that? | MOH-zhesh pof-TOO-zhihch |
| Jak to powiedzieć po polsku? | How do you say this in Polish? | yak toh poh-VYEH-jetch poh POL-skoo |
| Popraw mnie, proszę. | Correct me, please. | POH-praf mnyeh PROH-sheh |
| Czy tak jest dobrze? | Is it right like this? | chih tak yest DOB-zheh |

## Quick Prompts
1. Ask her to repeat.
2. Ask her to say it slower.
3. Say you do not understand yet.
4. Ask how to say "I need five minutes" in Polish.
5. Ask if your phrase is correct.

## Speaking Task
Have a two-minute exchange where you ask at least one repair question in Polish.

## Use Today
Ask for one correction in Polish and record it.

## Review Cards

# Phrasebook: Home and Family Starter

## Context
First set of phrases for daily family use at home.

| Polish | English | Pronunciation | Status | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Kocham cię. | I love you. | KOH-ham chyeh | new | Daily |
| Pomóż mi, proszę. | Help me, please. | POH-moosh mee PROH-sheh | new | Useful request |
| Posprzątajmy razem. | Let’s clean up together. | poh-spzhon-TAI-mih RAH-zem | new | Cleanup |
| Czas iść. | Time to go. | chahs eeshch | new | Leaving |
| Chcesz wody? | Do you want water? | hchesh VO-dih | new | Kitchen |
| Co się stało? | What happened? | tsoh shyeh STAH-woh | new | Feelings |
| Możesz powtórzyć? | Can you repeat that? | MOH-zhesh pof-TOO-zhihch | new | Repair |
| Próbuję mówić więcej po polsku. | I’m trying to speak more Polish. | PROO-boo-yeh MOO-veech VYEN-tsei poh POL-skoo | new | Wife |

## Useful Variations
- Chcesz mleka? = Do you want milk?
- Chcesz coś zjeść? = Do you want something to eat?
- Możesz powiedzieć wolniej? = Can you say it slower?

## Practice Prompts
- Ask for water.
- Tell a child it is time to go.
- Ask your wife to repeat something.
